About Us

A Gamefied Homework Forum

Website name is an online, free-to-use platform that transforms the common help forum into a learning game. Giving out helpful responses allows users to climb up the leaderboard through the accumulation of gold, an in-game currency. However, the quality of a response is judged by the questioner themselves which invites friendly competition as well as the need for field expertise.
